Executive Summary

Find your niche.

The key decisions this document makes on behalf of the brand. Everything else descends from these.

Primary logo OPT 02 — bold rounded humanist, tilted N. Best balance of warmth, distinctiveness, and 10-year longevity for a 3-pillar institute.
Submark "N." house-mark — promoted to system-canonical. The period punctuates a carved niche. It will outlive the wordmark as favicon, app icon, embroidery.
OPT 01 role Retained as NICHE Labs / Tech / AI sub-brand visual language — its industrial authority is correct for hackathons and technical programs.
OPT 03 role Reframed as NICHE Journal / Annual editorial masthead — not retired, scoped. Fails embroidery and 16px tests at institutional scale.
Type pairing Fraunces (free display, GT Sectra substitute until licensed) + Bai Jamjuree (Thai+Latin UI/body, free SIL OFL). See §6.
Color system Hold at 5 brand colors. Red is display-only — never body text. Warm gray is decorative-only — never text. Green: body only on dark background.
Voice register Declarative. Provocative. Short. "Are you learning wrong way?" — challenge the system, never the person. Side with the independent. See §8.
Critical gaps Motion system, AI-content policy, WCAG audit, dark-mode tokens, partnership lockups, governance — all addressed in this document for the first time.

"Are you learning wrong way?"

NICHE's voice is three words or a direct challenge. Not an elevator pitch. Not a vision statement. A provocation that earns its place.
