Section 15

Event & Wayfinding

NICHE in physical space at events and the November cohort venue.

Overview

Stage backdrop

Full-bleed #252026 background. NICHE wordmark (OPT 02, off-white) centered at maximum readable scale for the stage width. Tagline below in Fraunces. No photography — the type is the visual.

Pull-up banner

Pull-up banner

800mm × 2000mm retractable banner. Vertical layout: N. submark at top, wordmark below, tagline, program list in Bai Jamjuree. Bottom third: cohort dates + URL. Background: #252026.

Event badge/lanyard

Event badge/lanyard

86 × 54mm card. Front: participant name (Fraunces Bold 20pt), program (Bai Jamjuree), N. submark bottom-right. Back: NICHE wordmark + schedule QR code. Background: #f2f1f0.

Environmental signage scale guide

Environmental signage scale guide

Minimum letter height for environmental signage: 25mm per meter of viewing distance. At 5m viewing: 125mm cap height minimum. OPT 02 recommended at all signage scales.
