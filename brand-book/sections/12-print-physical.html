Section 12

Print & Physical Touchpoints

The brand in the physical world. Brochure, billboard, namecard, t-shirt — each with its own rules for layout, scale, and color.

Brochure

The most detailed touchpoint. The whole system in one artifact.

Page 8 — Brochure (left) and document folder (right). The folder uses full-bleed red with the N. submark and course label in off-white — maximum brand confidence.
Format A5 (148 × 210mm) saddlestitch or A4 (210 × 297mm) landscape fold
Cover Full-bleed photography. NICHE wordmark (OPT 02) in off-white, bottom-left. Tagline optional.
Inside spread 3mm margin. Bai Jamjuree 18pt body. Fraunces 36–56pt section heads. Max 600 characters per spread.
Color mode CMYK (see §5 for values). Spot color option for red: Pantone 485 C.
Paper Cover: 300gsm uncoated (matches brand's matte, not glossy, identity). Inside: 130gsm uncoated.
Billboard & Signage

Five seconds. Three words.

Billboard viewers have 5 seconds. NICHE's billboard copy was born for this: "Find your niche" is 3 words. "Are you learning wrong way?" is 6 words. Nothing needs to be longer than that.

Page 10 — Bus-stop + post signage. Note: OPT 03 thin wordmark is used here — acceptable at large billboard scale, not at namecard scale.
Page 13 — Full billboard. Photography occupies full bleed. Type layer on top. Negative space preserved for legibility.
Standard billboard (6×3m) Fraunces Bold at 600mm+ cap height. Maximum 6 words. Wordmark OPT 02 at 400mm minimum.
Bus stop / backlit (1.2×1.8m) OPT 02 or OPT 03 both acceptable (large enough for thin strokes). 4mm minimum stroke width after reduction.
Safe margin 150mm minimum from edge on 6×3m. Scale proportionally for smaller formats.
Photography on billboard Mode 1 or Mode 2 (see §7). Full bleed. Ensure 20% clear area in upper or left third for type layer.
Resolution 72dpi at final print size for large format. Supply at 1:10 ratio: 720dpi at A4 = correct for 6×3m print.
Namecards

Three card designs. One per logo option.

Each namecard uses a different logo option, allowing stakeholders to carry the card corresponding to their program's sub-brand. Dark background only — the brand owns the night.

Page 14 — Three namecard designs. All on #252026. Left: OPT 01 (Labs). Center: OPT 02 (Institutional). Right: OPT 03 (Journal). Each maps to a sub-brand context.
Size Standard 90 × 55mm (ISO 7810 ID-1)
Background #252026 (near-black) only. No light-background namecard.
Text color Name: #f2f1f0. Role + info: rgba(242,241,240,0.6).
Logo size OPT 02 wordmark at 35mm wide. N. submark at 8mm. Both within clear-space rules.
Typeface Bai Jamjuree only — no Fraunces on namecard (too large-display at card scale). Name: 9pt. Info: 7pt.
Finish Matte lamination on front and back. Spot UV on logo only (optional). No glossy.
T-Shirt Placement

Back of shirt. Center. Logo only.

NICHE t-shirts follow the principle of confident restraint. One logo. Back only. No slogans on the chest. The logo speaks.

Page 17 — Three t-shirts, one per logo. White shirt, dark logo, back placement. The green background in this photo is staging only — shirts are white/natural.
Position Back center. 100mm from collar. Logo centered horizontally.
Print size OPT 02 wordmark at 200mm wide. OPT 01 at 200mm. OPT 03 at 180mm (thinner strokes need slightly smaller to prevent bleed).
Shirt color Off-white / natural (closest to #f2f1f0) — not pure white, not gray.
Print method Screen print preferred. DTG acceptable for small runs. No heat transfer vinyl — edges lift.
Ink color #252026 (near-black). Single color. No red on shirt — too aggressive as a wearable.
Minimum stroke for OPT 03 OPT 03 must not print below 180mm wide — strokes too thin for screen print at smaller sizes. Use OPT 02 below this.
Poster System

Three-panel. One brand block, one photo, one date.

Page 12 — The NICHE poster system. Three panels: pure brand color (left), photography with headline (center), date + event (right). System works individually and as a triptych.
Format A1 (594 × 841mm) portrait. Three panels displayed together or individually.
Panel 1 — Brand block Full-bleed brand color (#fe1d25 or #252026). NICHE wordmark (OPT 02) in off-white, center. No other elements.
Panel 2 — Photo + headline Full-bleed photography (Mode 2 preferred). Fraunces headline overlay: "Find your niche" or a challenge hook. Logo bottom-right.
Panel 3 — Date + photo Photography with date, cohort detail, and URL. Bai Jamjuree 28pt for date. URL in eyebrow style. Logo bottom-left.
Composite Layout System

The full color system at work.

Page 18 from the original brief shows the composite layout system — all five brand colors in a multi-tile grid composition. This is the most ambitious application of the NICHE identity.

Page 18 — The NICHE composite layout. Red "Are you learning wrong way?", off-white URL, green "Program" pill, gray "Find your niche" display, off-white N. submark, program list, red full-name block. All on #252026 base.
Background #252026 — the near-black unifies all tiles
Tile proportions No equal-size grid. Tiles vary: 2:1, 1:1, 3:2. Asymmetry is intentional — editorial, not decorative.
Red tile rule Red (#fe1d25) tiles occupy maximum 30% of composite area. More than 30% and the red loses its impact.
Typography in tiles Each tile uses only one typeface: display tiles → Fraunces. Info tiles → Bai Jamjuree. Never both in the same tile.
Print Production Specs

What to send to your printer. No exceptions.

File format PDF/X-1a or PDF/X-4 (PDF print standard). All fonts outlined. No RGB — CMYK only.
Bleed 3mm bleed on all edges. Crop marks included. No slug required.
Color profile ISO Coated v2 300% (ECI) for coated stock. ISO Uncoated v2 for uncoated stock (brochure, namecard).
Image resolution 300dpi minimum at final print size. Embed all images.
Black Rich black for large fills: C60 M40 Y40 K100. Pure K100 for small text only. Never use #252026 directly — convert to CMYK.
Overprint check Verify overprint settings in Acrobat. Black text must be set to overprint ON. Colored fills must be set to overprint OFF.
