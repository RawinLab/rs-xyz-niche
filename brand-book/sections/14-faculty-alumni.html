Section 14

Faculty, Staff & Alumni

How people who represent NICHE are presented.

Overview

Headshot specification

All faculty and staff headshots use Mode 1 photography (see §7): environmental context, mid-task or at workspace. No studio portrait style. No headshots against white walls. The person is doing their actual work.

Bio lengths

Bio lengths

Short bio: 60–80 words. Long bio: 200–250 words. Both in Bai Jamjuree Regular, #252026 on #f2f1f0. Headers in Fraunces. No 'passionate about' language (see §8 forbidden words).

NICHE Alumni badge

NICHE Alumni badge

Digital badge: 'NICHE Alumni — [Year] Cohort'. N. submark + cohort year. Off-white on near-black. 400×400px for social sharing.

UGC repost rules

UGC repost rules

User-generated content may be reposted to NICHE's IG with explicit written credit: '@username / used with permission'. Never crop out the original creator's handle. Never repost without permission.
