Section 20

Governance & Version Log

Who maintains the brand, how it evolves, and when it gets reviewed.

Overview

Brand steward role

One named person holds final authority over brand decisions. Not a committee. Committees produce compromise — brands need conviction. The brand steward approves: new templates, partner lockups, campaign deviations, color additions, and any changes to the logo system.

Approval process

Approval process

New brand application: request → brief → design → steward review (48h SLA) → approved/revise → published. Emergency approvals (same-day): brand steward only, logged in version notes.

Change request template

Change request template

Any proposed change to this document must include: what is changing, why, what problem it solves, what it breaks, and who requested it. Changes that compromise the accessibility audit (§16) require additional justification.

Review cadence

Review cadence

Annual review: every November, one month before the next cohort open. Review covers: type license status, color system, new touchpoints from the past year, and accessibility re-audit.
