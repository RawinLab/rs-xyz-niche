Section 13

Course Card & Certificate System

The templates that every program needs before launch.

Overview

Course card principle

The course card is the primary marketing unit for each program. It must work at 400px wide (web card), 1080×1080 (IG post), and A5 print simultaneously. The system uses a grid of: program name (Fraunces), pillar label (eyebrow), dates, duration, and a hook sentence.

Certificate principle

Certificate principle

Certificates use formal register: Fraunces Bold for the recipient's name, Bai Jamjuree for institutional details. The N. submark replaces the wordmark at embossing / foil scale. The certificate must survive 10 years without looking dated.

Micro-credential badge

Micro-credential badge

A 400×400px digital badge for social sharing. N. submark + program name + pillar color chip. No photographs. The badge must read at 100×100px thumbnail size.

Pending Content

Brand owner input required.

Course card Figma template: Build the course card as a Figma component with text/image layer controls. All 12 program variants should be derivable from one master component.

Certificate template: Build the certificate in both A4 landscape and A4 portrait. Decide: is the NICHE seal embossed or foiled on the physical print? Foil requires a separate spot-UV plate — add cost to print budget.
