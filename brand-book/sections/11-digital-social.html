Digital & Social Templates

IG is NICHE's primary digital presence before launch. Every template must look like the brand built it — not like Canva picked a color.

Three story modes from the original brief.

These are the existing IG Story templates from the brand PDF. They show the system working — block-color tiles, typography overlay, and multi-section layouts. Study the proportions before designing new templates.

IG Post — Reference

Square. Tight. No wasted space.

Canvas size 1080 × 1080px (1:1 square)

Grid format (carousel) 3-column feed grid: alternate block-color / photography / typography posts. Never two consecutive photography posts.

Email Signature

Text-only. No image logos in signatures.

HTML email logo images are frequently blocked by corporate mail clients. NICHE signatures use text + the "N." submark as an inline HTML character, not an image file. This ensures delivery.

[Full Name]
[Role] · NICHE
New Institute of Creativity, Humanity & Entrepreneurship
www.niche.com · @niche.official
Font Use system fonts only in email: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif. Bai Jamjuree is not supported in Outlook/Gmail.
Color #252026 for name. rgba(37,32,38,0.6) for role. No red — too aggressive in every email.
No images Do not embed the logo as an image. Many clients block images by default. Name as text is always visible.
Social links www.niche.com and @niche.official only. No LinkedIn, Facebook or other platform links in signature.
