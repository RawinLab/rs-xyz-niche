Learning Objectives

  • Understand how typeface selection communicates personality and cultural context
  • Apply type pairing principles to a personal identity system
  • Analyse 3 brand case studies through the lens of typographic identity

Session Overview

Typography is not decoration — it is the voice of a brand made visible. In this session we move beyond readability into the territory of character: how a typeface family encodes history, emotion, and aspiration into every letterform.

We will examine how three Bangkok-based brands made deliberate typographic choices that reflect their founders' worldview, and then apply those learnings to your own ongoing identity project.
