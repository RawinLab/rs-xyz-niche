Semiotic Analysis

Deconstruct a brand using semiotic theory.

Needs revision Due Fri 14 Nov · 23:59

Assignment brief

Assigned by Karnwipa Jantara · Mon 9 Nov
Download PDF

Select a Thai or international brand identity and conduct a structured semiotic analysis. Your analysis should reveal how signs, symbols, and visual codes work together to construct meaning — and for whom.

Your submission must include both written analysis and a visual board that illustrates your key semiotic arguments.

Deliverables
  • Written analysis: 800–1,200 words (PDF or Google Doc link)
  • Visual board: A3 size, annotated, exported as PNG or PDF
  • Brand reference list: minimum 3 secondary sources
