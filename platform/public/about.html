The three pillars

What we actually mean.

01

Creativity

Not art for its own sake. Creativity is the capacity to make things that other people haven't made yet — in business, in product, in content, in culture. The programs in this pillar teach you to find and defend a distinct creative identity, then build systems that let you operate at scale without losing it.

"Your aesthetic is the business model."

02

Humanity

The industries that require you to actually understand people — hospitality, food, events, community. These are not dying sectors. They are the ones most resistant to commoditization because human judgment cannot be automated out. The programs here teach the craft and business of industries built on experience.

"What cannot be automated cannot be commoditized."

03

Entrepreneurship

Not startup theater. Entrepreneurship means building something that generates revenue, serves real people, and survives. The programs here are practical to the point of discomfort — you leave with a working business or a working skill set that gets you there faster.

"Revenue is evidence. Everything else is theory."

The story

Why we exist.

Southeast Asia is producing a generation of independent builders that has no institution designed for them. Every business school is optimized for corporate placement. Every design school is optimized for agency jobs. Every coding bootcamp is optimized for employment at a tech company.

NICHE is optimized for something different: building an independent. Someone who controls their own work, their own income, and their own creative direction.

We run one cohort per year because peer learning is the model and peer learning requires depth of relationship that rolling enrollment destroys. When you spend three to twelve months in a cohort of eleven people, something different happens. You stop performing and start working.

Bangkok is intentional. It is one of the most dynamic creative and entrepreneurial ecosystems in Southeast Asia, with a depth of craft, food, hospitality, and design culture that makes it a genuinely useful context for all three pillars.
