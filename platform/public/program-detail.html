Curriculum overview

What you'll build.

01

Voice audit & platform mapping

Map where your voice currently lives, where it should live, and what the gap costs you. You'll document your creative identity before we touch any tools.

Weeks 1–3

02

AI workflow design

Build your first AI-assisted production pipeline. Not prompts — systems. Ingestion, generation, editing, and publishing, all wired together.

Weeks 4–8

03

Distribution & audience architecture

A content engine without a distribution strategy is just noise. You'll map audience psychology, platform algorithms, and build the feedback loops that compound.

Weeks 9–14

04

Monetization & ownership

IP strategy, licensing, subscription architectures, brand deals — how to turn creative output into recurring revenue without losing control of your work.

Weeks 15–19

Live engine & graduation review
